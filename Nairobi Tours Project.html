What Makes Your Experience Special


Experience Nairobi heart like a true insider with a curated adventure that blends wildlife, culture, and unforgettable cuisine. We'll start at dawn in Nairobi National Park, just 7km from the city center, where you'll spot lions, leopards, and rhinos in their natural habitat. Then, meet the baby elephants at Sheldrick Wildlife Trust—I've known the keepers for years, and their heartfelt stories will stay with you forever. From there, we'll dive into the city's rich culture: explore Karen Blixen’s historic home, shop like a local at the vibrant Maasai Market (where my grandmother taught me the art of bargaining), and skip the tourist traps for authentic experiences. We'll cap it off with a food journey through Nairobi—think sizzling nyama choma at Carnivore, soulful ugali in Kibera, and the most flavorful samosas in Eastleigh’s Little Mogadishu. This is your Nairobi, raw and real—just the way it should be.
